ABOUT ME

I am a third year computer engineering student at the University of Waterloo. My recent passion for minimalistic but aesthetically pleasing user interface has led to the development of my personal website. Although many University students seek the seemingly ludicrous aspects to campus life, I am contrarily in a thirst for scholarship that shuns mediocrity. As an individual who constantly seeks the acquisition of knowledge, I am currently pursuing primary proficiency in Android Development, a path I hope to make a career in. The zest and energy that drives my academic and vocational quests prove my resilience because I view work as pleasure and pleasure as work. Like Confucius asserts, "do a job you love and you will never have to work a day in your life."
